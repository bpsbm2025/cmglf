How It Works

1

Request Intake Forms

Submit your information and you will receieve a packet of intake forms to begin your estate planning.

2

Complete Intake Forms

Fill out the intake forms and return the completed forms via U.S. Mail. We will use this information to create the first draft of your estate plan documents.

3

Review, Revise, and Approve

Schedule a time to review, revise, and approve your estate plan documents with legal counsel.

4

Finalize Estate Plan and Pay on Delivery

Pay and receive final copies of your estate plan documents.
